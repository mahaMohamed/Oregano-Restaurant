Our Story

It all began in 1990, when the Giovanni brothers settled in Egypt, and decided to bring their love for their homeland Italian cuisine to the streets of Cairo. They established "Oregano", and the rest is history.

Unparalleled Dining Experience

Like a timepiece, our Italian run kitchens and our qualified chefs, winners of many coveted awards, work to provide you with the freshest, tastiest cuisines at international standards. With beautiful surroundings, enchanting ambience, and heavenly tasting gourmet food, we provide you with unparalleled dining experience.
